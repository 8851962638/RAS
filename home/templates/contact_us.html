Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of artistic services including custom paintings, murals, digital art, portraits, and art workshops. We also provide bulk orders for corporate and event needs with special pricing.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and size. Typically, custom paintings take 2-4 weeks, murals 1-2 weeks, and portraits 1-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Whether you need multiple pieces for an event, corporate gifting, or large-scale projects, contact us for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. For large orders, we also offer flexible payment plans.

Can I visit your studio?

Absolutely! We welcome studio visits. Please schedule an appointment in advance so we can give you our full attention and show you our portfolio and ongoing projects.

Do you ship artwork internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! We use professional art packaging and reliable shipping partners to ensure your artwork arrives safely. Shipping costs vary based on destination and artwork size.
